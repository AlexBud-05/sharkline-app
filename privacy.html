🦈 Privacy Policy for Sharkline

Last updated: 10.04.2026

1. Introduction

Sharkline is a productivity application designed to help users track deadlines for school, work, and personal projects.

Your privacy is important. This app is designed with a strong focus on simplicity and data minimization.

2. Data Collection

Sharkline does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All data you enter into the app (such as tasks, deadlines, or notes) is stored locally on your device only.

3. Data Usage

Your data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app:

  • Tracking deadlines
  • Managing tasks and projects
  • Displaying reminders (if implemented)

No data is used for analytics, advertising, or profiling.

4. Data Sharing

Sharkline does not share your data with any third parties.

There are no external servers, no cloud synchronization, and no third-party integrations.

5. Data Security

  • You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device
  • Sharkline does not have access to your data

6. Internet Access

Sharkline does not require an internet connection to function.

If future versions introduce online features, this policy will be updated accordingly.

7. Children's Privacy

Sharkline does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be reflected with an updated date.
